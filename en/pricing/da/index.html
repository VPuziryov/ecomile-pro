Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to start with the test?

No. You can begin with the full launch or immediately run campaigns inside your own Business Manager.

Who pays the Meta advertising budget?

For all ongoing packages, the Meta advertising budget is paid separately. The only exception is the Test Launch, where a small advertising budget is already included.

Can I move everything to my own Business Manager later?

Absolutely. Migration can be done at any time. After three months of continuous cooperation, it is free of charge.

What is NOT included in monthly management?

Creating new advertising creatives, landing pages, Meta Pixel, Conversion API, chatbots, websites and other custom development.
