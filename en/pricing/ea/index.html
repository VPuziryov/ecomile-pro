Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the setup fee?

Preparation of the advertising infrastructure, setup of Business Manager and the advertising account, creation of the first advertising campaign, an initial set of advertising materials, campaign launch, optimization, and the first month of management.

Who pays the advertising budget?

The advertising budget is not included in either the setup fee or the monthly management fee. It is paid separately directly to the advertising platform.

Do I have to continue monthly management?

No. The first month of management is already included in the setup fee. After it ends, you decide whether to continue working with EasyAds or manage the advertising yourself.

Is EasyAds suitable for my business?

EasyAds is designed for small businesses that need a clear, predictable, and professional advertising launch without having to learn Meta Ads themselves.
