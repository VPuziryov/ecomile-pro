What matters in diving

Diving rarely sells on the first click.

  • Decisions are gradual.
  • Trust matters more than discounts.
  • Location and season are critical.
  • Often a course or safari is sold — not a single dive.

Advertising must reflect these realities, otherwise stability is impossible.
