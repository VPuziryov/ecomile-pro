Industry Reality

Diving is not an impulse purchase.

  • Trust outweighs price
  • Decision cycles are longer
  • Seasonality directly affects demand
  • Often courses or trips are sold — not single dives

Advertising must reflect these dynamics. Otherwise it becomes expensive and unstable.
